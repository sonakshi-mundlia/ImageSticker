Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is StickSpark?

StickSpark is an AI tool that instantly turns your photos into custom, artistic stickers with automatic background removal.

Are my photos kept private?

Yes; we use an "Instant Purge" policy where original photos are deleted immediately after your sticker is generated.

Do you sell my personal data?

Never; we have a strict "No-Sale" policy and do not monetize your photos or facial data with third-party advertisers.

What artistic styles can I choose?

You can choose from several modes, including Face Focus, Full-Body, and complete Cartoon Overhaul.

Who owns the stickers I create?

You do; you retain full ownership of any stickers generated by the engine.

How do I use my stickers?

You can download them as transparent PNGs or export them directly to apps like WhatsApp and Telegram.
