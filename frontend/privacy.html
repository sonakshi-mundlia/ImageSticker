Transient and Purpose-Bound Image Processing

When you upload a photo for sticker generation, it is transmitted to our secure servers solely for the purpose of AI transformation. This ensures that your original data is only handled during the active creation phase and is never diverted for other uses, such as background data harvesting, hidden profiling, or unauthorized secondary analysis.

Instant and Permanent Auto-Deletion Policy

To maintain a secure environment, original source images are automatically purged from our temporary processing cache as soon as your sticker is generated. This leaves no permanent footprint of your source file on our infrastructure, which fundamentally minimizes the risk of unauthorized access or data leaks involving your personal imagery. Once the "Spark" engine completes the final render, the raw input is wiped using industry-standard deletion protocols.

Complete and Irrevocable User Ownership

Every sticker you create remains your property, and we do not maintain a permanent gallery of your personal uploads on our servers. Once you download your sticker, you have full control over the final creative content and how it is shared across digital platforms. We do not claim any intellectual property rights over your transformations, ensuring that your creative work—and the likeness within it—belongs strictly to you.

Creative Mathematical Geometry vs. Biometrics

Our AI utilizes mathematical mapping of facial features and body proportions to apply artistic styles rather than for biometric identification. This approach allows for sophisticated stylization—like cartoon or 3D renders—without the need for tracking, identifying, or storing sensitive identity markers. We interpret the "geometry" of a photo to understand where to place borders and art effects, rather than attempting to recognize the individual.

Precision Likeness and Personality Preservation

Proportion and feature analysis are used strictly as creative tools to ensure that the chosen "vibe" accurately captures your unique essence. This preserves your personality while shifting the image into a new artistic medium, ensuring the results are recognizable yet fully transformed. By analyzing the relationship between features, the AI can replicate your specific smile or pose in a cartoon style without needing to store any personal metadata.

Secure End-to-End SSL/TLS Transmission

All data is sent through encrypted SSL/TLS tunnels during transit between your device and our "Spark" engine. This creates a secure "pass-through" system that prevents any unauthorized third-party access, packet sniffing, or interception during the upload and processing cycle. We utilize the same level of encryption used by global financial institutions to ensure that your private photos stay private during their journey to our servers.

Strict Anti-Monetization and No-Sale Policy

We maintain a strict, non-negotiable policy that forbids the selling of your photos, facial data, or any personal information to third-party advertisers or data brokers. Our business model is built entirely on providing a high-quality creative service, not on the monetization of user data. We believe your digital identity is not a product, and we proactively block any third-party scripts that attempt to track user behavior for advertising purposes.

Essential-Only Infrastructure and Partner Compliance

Data is shared only with necessary technical infrastructure partners, such as secure cloud hosting providers, required to keep the platform functional. These partners are legally bound by strict confidentiality agreements and rigorous data protection standards that mirror our own. We conduct regular audits of our infrastructure partners to ensure they maintain the high-security environment required for real-time AI processing.

Full User Autonomy and Data Deletion Rights

You retain the absolute right to manage, access, or permanently delete your account data at any time through your user settings. We are committed to total transparency, giving you authority over your digital presence as technology and security standards evolve. If you choose to delete your account, all associated metadata and saved preferences are scrubbed from our systems entirely, leaving no residual data behind.
